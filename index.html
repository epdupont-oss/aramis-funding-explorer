⚠ Please read before using this tool

Personal project, no institutional affiliation. This is a hobby project built by a private individual with no connection to the Swiss federal administration, ARAMIS, SATW, or any research institution. It is not peer-reviewed, not officially endorsed, and should not be cited as an authoritative source.

The analysis is amateurish by design. Statistics, trends, and categorisations shown here are rough extrapolations from incomplete data. Around 10% of projects have no budget, ~60% have no location data, and SATW technology tags were assigned by a general-purpose AI model that makes systematic errors (e.g. "Industry 5.0" is almost certainly over-tagged). Funding totals and project counts may differ from official ARAMIS figures.

Do not base decisions on this tool. If you need reliable figures, go directly to aramis.admin.ch. Abstracts and keywords were generated by Mistral Small and may contain errors, mistranslations, or hallucinations. Always verify important details against the original source.
